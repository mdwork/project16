Новости

  • Engineers form our company have successfully completed the course in “Sperry Marine” / “Thrane & Thran e” external communication equipment 5000 series.

  • Our engineers has attended and successfully passed the course of inspection, testing, repairing & packing of “Seaman Safety” inflatable liferafts.

  • Our engineers have successfully completed the training course held in “Yuksel Makina” (Turkey) training facility regarding the operating, inspection, maintenance and repair of rescue boat / liferaft / provision cranes; freefall davit (launching and retrieval systems) and side launching (lifeboat) davits.

  • Through a qualification audit performed by “Germanischer Lloyd” on 2010-09-24, “Electric Engineering Ltd.” has been found capable to conduct service, maintenance and testing of launching appliances and lifeboat / rescue boat release gear, automatic release hooks & liferafts and release systems of freefall lifeboats as per SOLAS III, Reg. 20.11.1, 20.11.2 and 20.11.3.
